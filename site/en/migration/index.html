Migration FAQ

What people ask most often.

Which tools can I migrate from?

We most commonly move data from spreadsheets, simple client databases, and other interior design project management systems. The scope and format are agreed individually during the consultation.

How long does migration take?

It depends on the amount of data. After assessing the scope you receive a concrete schedule — most migrations are completed within a few business days.

Is data migration paid?

The cost depends on the scope and source of the data — exact terms are presented after the assessment during the consultation.

Will I lose access to my old tool during migration?

No. We plan the migration so as not to interrupt your current work — you switch to veedeck only when everything is ready.
